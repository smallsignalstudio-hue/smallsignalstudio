App: Sippin — hydration reminders & logging Developer: Small Signal Studio

Common Questions

How do I restore Premium?

Open Settings → Restore purchases. Use the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

Why are reminders not showing?

Check Settings → Notifications on your iPhone and ensure Sippin is allowed. Reminders only fire during your configured wake hours.

Does Sippin work offline?

Yes. Logging, history, streaks, and reminders work without an internet connection. Weather-based goals need a network connection when enabled.

How do I delete my data?

Remove the app from your device. All local data is deleted with the app. If you enabled Apple Health sync, you may also remove Sippin’s entries in the Health app.
